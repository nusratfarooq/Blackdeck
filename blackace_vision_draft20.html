Structure & Geography

BlackAce is built as a dual-country institution — anchored in India, with a full operational and legal presence in the United States. The India entity is established first; the US entity follows. Our American partner firm, effectivRAI (eRAI), operates as our US-facing vehicle — and over time, the two institutions merge into one seamless, bi-continental operation.

BlackAce India

The founding entity. Headquartered in India. Leads institutional relationships, sovereign advisory, infrastructure structuring and financial operations across South Asia and emerging markets.

BlackAce USA (5–10 Year Vision)

US legal entity, established after India. Enables access to US capital markets, defense advisory, government procurement and cross-border deal flow. Operates in tandem with BlackAce India.

effectivRAI (eRAI)

A fully American AI applications firm — 50% co-owned by BlackAce co-founders. eRAI operates at the applications layer of the AI stack and would serve as BlackAce's US-market entry vehicle.

The Merged Institution

As BlackAce matures institutionally and establishes a strong India-US operating presence, BlackAce and eRAI can evolve into a closely integrated institution, combining infrastructure and application layer capabilities, institutional credibility and transatlantic reach.

We are not building for valuation. We are building for relevance, resilience and long-term institutional value.
